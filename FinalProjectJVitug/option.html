Our Options

These are our options to obtain our vehicles

Delivery

The Sedan has an option to be delivered to you. The cost is extra but car will be delivered to you with a full tank of gas. You must bring it back to our address at the end of your ride with a full tank of gas.

Pickup

Sedans and Vans(only option) have the option for pickup. There is no extra cost and also must be delivered back with a full tank of gas.

Meetup

Buses are only available for meetup. Buses will meet you at specific meetup points. Passengers will be dropped off at meetup point at end of ride.
